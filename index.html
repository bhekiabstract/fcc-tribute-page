Nipsey Hussle Facts

  • When 2013’s mixtape, Crenshaw, came out, Nipsey Hussle sold it at $100 per mixtape and netted $100,000 in the first 24 hours! Jay Z bought 100 copies to show his support.
  • He was nominated for Best Rap Album at the 61st Annual Grammy Awards in 2019.
  • He was of Eritrean and American descent.
  • Nipsey Hussle was also a member of the local Rollin 60’s Neighborhood Crips gang.
  • His stage name, a play on the name of comic Nipsey Russell.
  • Nipsey Hussle's Foundation financed the development of South Central L.A.’s Vector 90, a coworking space and incubator focused on developing underrepresented entrepreneurs.
  • Showing off his entrepreneur skills, in 2017 Nipsey Hussle, his older brother and a business partner, opened up The Marathon Clothing smart-store.
  • He was in a relationship with well known actor Lauren London.
  • At the time of hos death, he had two children Emani and Kross.
  • The last song he released before his untimely death was Racks in the middle.
  • His first posthumous verses were on a DJ Khaled song Higher with John Legend singing the hook.
